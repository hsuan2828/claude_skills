Search multiple job platforms for Product Manager / 產品經理 openings.

**Usage:**
- `/pm-jobs` — search all platforms with default settings
- `/pm-jobs fintech` — search and filter by keyword
- `/pm-jobs remote` — filter for remote-only positions
- `/pm-jobs senior` — filter for senior/lead roles

**Platforms searched:** CakeResume · LinkedIn · 104人力銀行 · Yourator · 1111人力銀行

Results are cross-checked against your existing job tracker to avoid duplicates. You'll be prompted to choose which jobs to save.

Use the pm-job-search skill to execute the search based on the arguments provided.
